Dr. Connor S. Kenaston is an Assistant Professor of History at Randolph College. His research focuses on 20th-century social and cultural history. , particularly religion, culture, and politics. Connor received his PhD from the University of Virginia in 2022, and his scholarship has been featured in publications such as Modern American History,” Hybrid Pedagogy and Methodist Review. He is currently working on a history of religious broadcasting, tentatively titled “Spirit of Power: Radio, Religion, and the Sound of the American Century.”
